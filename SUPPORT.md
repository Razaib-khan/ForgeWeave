## Getting Help

| Need | Where to go |
|---|---|
| **Questions about usage** | [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/Razaib-khan/forgeweave/discussions) |
| **Bug reports** | [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/Razaib-khan/forgeweave/issues) (use Bug Report template) |
| **Feature requests** | [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/Razaib-khan/forgeweave/issues) (use Feature Proposal template) |
| **Security vulnerabilities** | See [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) — do not open public issues |
| **Real-time chat** | Not yet available |

---

## Before Asking

1. **Search first** — check existing [issues](https://github.com/Razaib-khan/forgeweave/issues) and [discussions](https://github.com/Razaib-khan/forgeweave/discussions) for answers.
2. **Read the docs** — most questions are answered in the documents listed above.
3. **Be specific** — include your environment details, what you're trying to do, and what went wrong.

---

## Commercial Support

Commercial support is not currently available. ForgeWeave is a community-driven open-source project.
